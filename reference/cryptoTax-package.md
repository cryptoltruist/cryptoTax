# cryptoTax: Report Crypto Taxes (Canada Only)

Helps calculate crypto taxes in R. First, by allowing you to format .CSV
files from various exchanges to one large data frame of organized
transactions. Second, by allowing you to calculate your Adjusted Cost
Base (ACB), ACB per share, and realized and unrealized capital
gains/losses. Third, by calculating revenues gained from staking,
interest, airdrops, etc. Fourth, by calculating superficial losses as
well. \*Disclaimer: This is not financial advice. Use at your own risks.
There are no guarantees whatsoever in relation to the use of this
package. Please consult a tax professional as necessary\*.
